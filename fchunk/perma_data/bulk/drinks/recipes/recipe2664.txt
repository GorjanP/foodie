Place 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream into a blender; blend until smooth. Add strawberry syrup; blend until evenly distributed, about 1 minute. Add strawberries; blend for 1 minute. Pour mixture into a bowl.    
Place remaining 3 scoops of vanilla ice cream into a blender; blend until smooth. Add chocolate syrup; blend until evenly distributed, about 1 minute. Add bananas; blend for 30 seconds. Pour strawberry mixture back into the blender; blend together, about 30 seconds.    
Divide mixture evenly among 3 glasses.
