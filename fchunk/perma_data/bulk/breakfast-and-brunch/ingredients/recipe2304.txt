Crust:
3 cups shredded carrots
1/4 cup canola oil
1/4 cup diced onion
1 teaspoon salt substitute (such as No Salt®)
1 teaspoon ground black pepper
1/4 cup cold water
2 tablespoons cornstarch
Filling:
2 small heads broccoli, shredded
3 eggs
1/2 cup pesto sauce
1 teaspoon salt substitute (such as No Salt®)
Topping:
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese, or to taste
