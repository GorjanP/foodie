Bacon Alfredo Sauce:
6 slices Farmland® Thick Cut Bacon, diced
2 ounces butter
2 cloves garlic, sliced
1/4 cup all-purpose flour
1 cup heavy cream
1 cup fresh spinach
1 cup grated Parmesan cheese, divided
4 eggs
4 slices toasted sliced bread of choice, for dipping
Salt and pepper, to taste
