Cake:
2 cups white sugar
2 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1 cup water
1/2 cup vegetable shortening
1/2 cup margarine
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup buttermilk
2 eggs, beaten
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
Icing:
1 (16 ounce) box confectioners' sugar
3 tablespoons unsweetened cocoa powder
1/2 cup butter
6 tablespoons milk
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
1 cup chopped pecans
