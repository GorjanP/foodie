Wet ingredients:
2 tablespoons freshly grated orange zest
1/2 cup butter, melted
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1 tablespoon orange juice
1 tablespoon milk
1/4 teaspoon vanilla extract
Dry ingredients:
2 cups shelled dry-roasted pistachio nuts
1/2 teaspoon salt to taste (see footnote)
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 cup all-purpose flour
1/2 cup miniature chocolate chips (optional)
Glaze:
1 tablespoon orange juice, or as needed
2 teaspoons freshly grated orange zest
3 tablespoons confectioners' sugar, or as needed
