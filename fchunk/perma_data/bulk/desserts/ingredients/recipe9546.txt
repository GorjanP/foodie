Rhubarb Compote:
8 stalks rhubarb, cut into 1/2-inch pieces
3 apples - peeled, cored, and diced
1/2 cup white sugar
1/4 cup water
Cake:
2/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup margarine
1 egg
1 teaspoon vanilla extract
3/4 cup milk
1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
2 teaspoons baking powder
1/4 teaspoon salt
Crumb Topping:
1 1/2 cups rolled oats
1/2 cup margarine
1/2 cup white sugar
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
