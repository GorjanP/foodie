Syrup:
2 cups water
2 cups white sugar
4 whole cloves
1/2 cinnamon stick
1 lemon, juiced, divided
Filling:
1 pound finely chopped walnuts
1 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons ground cinnamon
1/2 teaspoon ground nutmeg
3/4 cup unsalted butter, melted, or more as needed
20 sheets phyllo dough
