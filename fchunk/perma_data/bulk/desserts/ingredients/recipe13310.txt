Crust:
2 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup solid coconut oil
2 tablespoons white sugar
1 pinch salt
1/4 cup ice water
Curd Filling:
2 cups white sugar
1 cup coconut cream
3 eggs
3 egg yolks
3 lemons, zested and juiced
3 tablespoons butter, softened
1 pinch salt
Meringue:
3 egg whites
1/2 cup white sugar
1 pinch salt
1 pinch cream of tartar (optional)
