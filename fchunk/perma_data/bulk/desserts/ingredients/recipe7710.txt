Biscotti:
3 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
1 teaspoon baking powder
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup butter, softened
1/2 cup white sugar
2 eggs
1/2 cup maple syrup
1 teaspoon maple extract
1 cup chopped walnuts
Maple Glaze:
1 tablespoon melted butter
1/4 cup maple syrup
3/4 cup confectioners' sugar
