Pie Crust:
2 cups flour
1/2 teaspoon salt
1/2 teaspoon baking powder
1 tablespoon sugar
8 ounces Finlandia Unsalted Butter, cold and cubed
1/2 cup sour cream, cold
Apple Filling:
6 medium-to-large apples, sliced thin
3 teaspoons ground cinnamon
3 tablespoons sugar
2 tablespoons apple juice or lemon juice
2 tablespoons Finlandia Unsalted Butter
Egg Wash:
1 egg
1 tablespoon water
