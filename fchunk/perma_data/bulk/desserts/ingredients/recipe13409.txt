Crust:
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1 cup whole wheat flour
1 cup almond meal
1 tablespoon white sugar
1 teaspoon lemon zest
1/2 teaspoon salt
3/4 cup butter
3 tablespoons buttermilk
Filling:
10 egg yolks
2/3 cup white sugar
2/3 cup cornstarch
1 quart milk
3 sprigs basil
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract
15 fresh apricots, pitted and sliced
1 cup apricot jam
