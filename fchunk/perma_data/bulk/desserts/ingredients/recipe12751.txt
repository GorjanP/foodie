Cake:
2 egg whites

2/3 cup white sugar
1 1/3 cups all-purpose flour

1 teaspoon salt
1/2 cup whole milk

Frosting:
1/3 cup butter, softened
1 1/2 cups confectioners' sugar
2 egg yolks
1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Topping:
3 (1 ounce) squares semisweet chocolate
2 tablespoons butter
