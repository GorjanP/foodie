1 recipe pastry for a 9 inch single crust pie

1/2 cup white sugar
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
1/2 cup molasses
1 egg
1 cup water
3 tablespoons fresh lemon juice

Top Layer:
2/3 cup white sugar
1/4 cup butter, room temperature
1 egg, beaten
1 1/4 cups all-purpose flour
1/2 teaspoon baking soda
1/2 cup buttermilk
