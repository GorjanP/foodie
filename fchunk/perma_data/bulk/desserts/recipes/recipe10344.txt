In a large bowl, cream together butter or margarine and sugar until light and fluffy.  Add the eggs one at a time, and beat well after each addition.    
Sift and measure flour, and reserve two tablespoons.  Mix remaining flour with soda and spices; stir into the creamed mixture.    
Dredge raisins in the 2 reserved tablespoons of flour, then stir them into the batter along with the walnuts.    
Drop dough by tablespoonfuls onto lightly greased baking sheet.  Bake at 375 degrees F (190 degrees C) for 8 to 10 minutes. Remove and cool cookies on a wire rack.
