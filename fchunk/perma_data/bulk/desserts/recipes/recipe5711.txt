Combine flour, cocoa, salt and baking powder in a medium bowl and stir until well-mixed.    
Whisk together sugar and oil in a large bowl. Add eggs and vanilla; whisk until blended. Add dry ingredients; whisk just until blended. Fold in chocolate chips.    
Pour batter into a lightly greased or a foil or parchment paper-lined 9-inch (23 cm) square baking pan; spread evenly.    
Bake in a preheated 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) oven until toothpick inserted near centre comes out clean, 25 to 30 minutes. Take care not to overbake brownies.
