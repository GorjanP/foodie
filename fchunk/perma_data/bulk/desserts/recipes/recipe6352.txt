Sift together flour, 1 cup sugar, salt, and baking soda.  Beat 2 eggs and pour over dry mixture.  Add fruit cocktail.  Stir together.    
Grease and flour pan.  Bake at 325 degrees F (165 degrees C) for 45 minutes.
