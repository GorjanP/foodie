Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).    
Combine flour, brown sugar, and egg in a large bowl. Cut in butter until mixture appears crumbly. Stir in chopped peanuts.    
Set 2 cups of the peanut crumble aside. Press remaining mixture into a 9x13-inch baking pan.    
Bake crust in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 15 minutes.    
Beat condensed milk, peanut butter, and vanilla extract together in a large bowl. Spread over the prepared crust. Top with reserved peanut crumble.    
Bake in the preheated oven until lightly browned, about 25 minutes. Cool before cutting into bars.
