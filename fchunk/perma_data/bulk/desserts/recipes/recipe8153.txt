Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease a 9 inch pie pan.    
Place eggs, salt, pumpkin, sugar, melted butter, baking mix, evaporated milk, and water in a blender. Mix on low speed for 2 minutes. Pour mixture into greased pie pan.    
Bake in preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, until set in center.
