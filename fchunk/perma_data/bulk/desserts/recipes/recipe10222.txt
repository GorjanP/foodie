Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Grease and flour a 9x9 inch pan.    
In a medium bowl, mix the flour, baking powder and salt. Set aside.    
In a large bowl, combine oil, eggs, sugar and milk. Beat until blended. Add flour mixture and beat until smooth. Fold in jam. Pour batter into 9x9 inch pan.    
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 30 to 35 minutes, or until toothpick inserted into center of cake comes out clean.
