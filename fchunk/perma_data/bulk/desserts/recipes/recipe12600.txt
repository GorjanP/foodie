Spread 2 Tbsp. peanut butter on each tortilla.    
On each tortilla, place 1/2 sliced banana on top of peanut butter. Then, top each with 1/2 cup of strawberries, pineapple and coconut.    
Fold in half.    
Place Edible Disney(R) Decals on Tortillas and enjoy (instructions on back of Mission Kid Size Tortilla package).
