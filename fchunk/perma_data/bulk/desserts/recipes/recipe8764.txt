Line a 9 x 9 inch pan with graham crackers.    
Boil together 1/2 cup butter or margarine, 1 cup brown sugar and scant 1/2 cup milk for two minutes.    
Remove from stove and stir in:  graham cracker crumbs, coconut, chopped walnuts,  and cherries.   Pour on graham crackers in pan.  Place a layer of graham crackers on top of filling.  Spread with Butter Icing.    
To Make Butter Icing:  Combine icing sugar, 3 tablespoons butter or margarine and approximately 3 tablespoons of milk, or amount needed to give a spreading  consistency.
