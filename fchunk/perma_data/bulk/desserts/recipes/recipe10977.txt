With electric mixer, beat sugar and egg yolk in medium bowl, 3 to 4 minutes.  Beat in whole egg until fluffy, 10 to 15 minutes more.  Beat in melted butter until smooth.  Stir in cocoa, crushed cookies and almonds.  Shape dough into a long sausage shape, wrap in waxed paper or plastic wrap and place on a baking sheet.  Freeze 4 hours, until firm.  Serve in thick slices.
