Whisk sugar, eggs, pineapple juice, lemon juice, and flour in a saucepan until smooth.    
Heat egg mixture over low heat, stirring constantly until thickened, about 10 minutes. Set aside to cool completely.    
Whisk milk and dessert topping in a large bowl until fluffy. Fold cooled egg mixture into dessert topping. Fold in fruit cocktail, pineapple chunks, grapes, and marshmallows.
