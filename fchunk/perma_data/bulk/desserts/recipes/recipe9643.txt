Preheat oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C).    
Cream shortening with vanilla.  Add sugars gradually, creaming well.  Add eggs and beat thoroughly.    
Sift flour, soda, and salt together; add to creamed mixture and mix well.  Stir in oats.    
Shape dough into balls and put on greased cookie sheets.  Make a hollow in each ball and place a gumdrop in each hollow.    
Bake for 10 to 12 minutes.
