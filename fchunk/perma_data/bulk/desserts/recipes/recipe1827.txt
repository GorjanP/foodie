Mix with a spoon (not an electric mixer) the cake mix, cherry pie filling, eggs, and almond extract  until moistened. Spread into 9 x 13-in greased pan.    
Bake at 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 25 to 30 minutes.    
To Make Frosting:    Bring sugar, butter, and milk to a boil. Boil for 1 minute.   Add chocolate chips; stir until chips are melted. Pour frosting over warm cake. Cool.
