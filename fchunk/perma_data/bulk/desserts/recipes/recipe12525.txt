Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).    
Melt chocolate over low heat.  Remove and cool.  Beat butter in small bowl to soften. Gradually beat in 2/3 cup confectioner's sugar, then 1 teaspoon vanilla and the melted chocolate.  Beat in 1 1/4 cups flour and 2 tablespoons milk.    
Shape a well-rounded teaspoon of dough around each chocolate kiss to cover completely.  Place cookies flat side down on greased cookie sheet.  Bake for 10-12 minutes or until lightly browned on bottom.  Frost cooled cookies.    
To Make Frosting:  Beat 2 tablespoons shortening till soft, gradually adding 1/2 cup confectioner's  sugar.  Mix well. Add 1 tablespoon milk and 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract.  Stir in additional confectioners' sugar (around 3/4 cup).  Frosting should be fluffy.
