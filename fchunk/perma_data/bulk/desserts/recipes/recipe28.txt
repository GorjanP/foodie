In a medium bowl, cream together the 1/2 cup of  butter and white sugar. Add pumpkin, egg, and 1 teaspoon vanilla to butter mixture, and beat until creamy. Mix in dry ingredients. Drop on cookie sheet by tablespoonfuls; flatten slightly.Watch Now    
Bake for 15 to 20 minutes in the preheated oven. Cool cookies, then drizzle glaze with fork.Watch Now
