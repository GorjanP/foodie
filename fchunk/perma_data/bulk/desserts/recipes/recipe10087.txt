Roll graham crackers to fine crumbs; there should be about 1 cup.  Melt the butter or margarine, and combine with the crumbs.  Pat into 9 inch pie plate.  Chill.    
Heat 24 to 30 large marshmallows slowly in the milk.  Cool.  Fold in whipped topping, vanilla and grated bitter chocolate.  Pour filling into crust, and refrigerate l hour.
