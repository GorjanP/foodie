Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (180 degrees C) and grease cookie sheets.    
Mix together flour, oatmeal, sugar, and coconut.    
Melt the butter or margarine, and combine with the water. Add the liquids to the dry ingredients.    
Place spoonfuls of mixture on tray well spaced.  Bake in center of oven for 15-20 minutes.
